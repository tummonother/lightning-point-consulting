## Selected Design Approach

**Neo-Corporate with Editorial Sensibility** (Response 3)

This approach best serves Lightning Point Consulting's positioning: credible, calm, professional yet human. The editorial sensibility aligns with their "slightly witty" voice while maintaining the gravitas needed for enterprise consulting. The magazine-style layout allows for the extensive service descriptions and stage-based architecture to breathe, while the refined interactions convey the "calm confidence" central to their brand.

The color blocking and varied layouts will help differentiate the four stages (Activate, Deliver, Run & Evolve, Specialty Services) while maintaining visual consistency. The gold accent bars and rounded corners soften what could otherwise be overly corporate, matching the "people-first" philosophy.
